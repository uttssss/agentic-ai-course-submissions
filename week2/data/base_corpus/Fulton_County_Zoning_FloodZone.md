# Fulton County, Georgia — Zoning and Flood Zone Disclosure Guidelines

## Flood Zone Designations
Properties in Fulton County are mapped under FEMA Flood Insurance Rate Maps (FIRM). Properties located in Special Flood Hazard Areas (Zones A and AE) require flood insurance when financed by a federally backed mortgage. Buyers should confirm the property's flood zone designation during the Due Diligence Period using the FEMA map service or a survey.

## Contractual Treatment of Flood Risk
Georgia purchase agreements typically address flood risk through the Property Condition and Disclosure provisions and any flood-zone special stipulation. If the agreement contains a flood-zone stipulation, the buyer may terminate within the Due Diligence Period if the property is found to be in a Special Flood Hazard Area. If no such stipulation is included, flood-zone discovery is handled under the buyer's general due diligence right to terminate.

## Zoning Compliance
Buyers should verify that the property's current use complies with its zoning classification. Non-conforming uses, pending variances, and easements are reviewable during the Due Diligence Period. The county does not warrant zoning compliance as part of any transaction.
