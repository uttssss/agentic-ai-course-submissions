# Executed Purchase and Sale Agreement — 482 Maplewood Lane, Atlanta, GA 30312

## Parties and Property
Buyer: Jordan Reyes. Seller: Pat Donovan. Property: 482 Maplewood Lane, Atlanta, Fulton County, GA 30312. Community: Maplewood Community Association (HOA).

## Key Dates and Terms
Binding Agreement Date: June 1, 2026.
Purchase Price: $415,000.
Earnest Money: $8,000, held in escrow by Peachtree Title.
Due Diligence Period: 10 days from the Binding Agreement Date.
Closing Date: July 15, 2026.

## Financing Contingency
This agreement INCLUDES a financing contingency. Loan amount: $373,500 (conventional). The buyer shall apply for financing within 5 days of the Binding Agreement Date. Final loan approval deadline: July 8, 2026. If final loan approval is not issued by that date, the buyer may terminate and recover earnest money.

## Appraisal Contingency
This agreement INCLUDES an appraisal contingency. If the appraised value is below $415,000, the buyer may terminate and recover earnest money, proceed at the contract price, or negotiate a price reduction.

## Inspection and Repairs
The home inspection and any written repair requests must be completed before the Due Diligence Period expires. The seller has agreed in Amendment 1 to repair the HVAC condenser and a roof flashing leak prior to closing.

## HOA Approval
This agreement INCLUDES an HOA-approval contingency tied to the Due Diligence Period. The buyer submitted the Maplewood Community Association application on June 2, 2026.

## Flood Zone
Special Stipulation 7: The property is represented as located in FEMA Zone X (minimal flood risk). The buyer may verify flood-zone status during the Due Diligence Period and terminate if the property is found to be in a Special Flood Hazard Area.

## Counteroffer Terms
The seller's most recent counteroffer specified that the buyer has 3 days to respond to any further counteroffer.
